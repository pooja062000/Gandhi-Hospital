About Us

Gandhi Hospital is 60-bedded secondary care hospital operating under the company name of Pawan Gandhi Healthcare Pvt. Ltd. Gandhi>Established in
1989 in West Delhi by Dr. Pawan Gandhi, thefacility has grown from a one room clinic to a nursing home and today to a state of the art multi
-specialty hospital with an aim of providing affordable, accessible, and quality healthcareto patients.

Our facility is equipped with 24*7 emergency services, dialysis units, pharmacy, fully automated labs including Cardiac Cath lab, modular
operation theatres, fully equipped adult and neonatal ICUs, and consultation in all general and super specialties such as cardiology, neurology,
nephrology, gastroenterology, and oncology amongst many others.

The hospital is operating on high-end healthcare technologies, smart digital systems, and efficient Hospital Information System to addres.
complex medical needs and provide comprehensive healthcare facilities to all sections of society under one roof.
