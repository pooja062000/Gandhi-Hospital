At Gandhi Hospital we aim to provide a single stop solution for investigations ranging from simple pathological tests to complex
problems multiple devices. We aim to ensure that all our services meet the clinical needs in the most affordable and
accessible fashion. We have a dedicated team of pathologist, biochemists, microbiologists, and other laboratory technicians which
enable the treating doctors in providing accurate and timely diagnosis. Our inhouse laboratory provides 24*7 coverage along-with home
sample collection.

We have a dedicated Cath Lab under the name of Charak Cardiac Centre in collaboration with Dr. Naresh Kumar Goyal, Senior Director,
Interventional Cardiology & In-charge Heart Failure Programme, at BLK-Max Super Specialty Hospital. Our Cath Lab is serviced
experienced cardiologists and technicians where in we provide both invasive and non-invasive cardiology services with a versatile and
patient-centric approach. We are one of the prime centers for angiography and angioplasty in South-West district of New Delhi.
